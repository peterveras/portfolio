Galaxy

UX/UI

The problem

The "Men in Black" like any other people on earth, also, do tedious office work, for such work they use Galaxy, an ancient system that once did everything; from looking for the bad guys to helping agents requesting new guns, or steplers. The once highly funded application is a shodow of itself. 10 years have passed by and no updates has been made to this product. Which has propted users to rely on a long list of work-arounds. Fortunately 2020 has brought new funding and the IT department has been tasked to updated this system.
Now the the problem is too many problems to fix

My Role

In this "ficticious role" I was the man, not one of the "Men in Black" but the only UX guy, so I was the whole team, I did the most I could. Anyway, BAs did the reasearch and I sat there nodding in a lot of meetings, I also helped setting up workshop and mapping workflows, I liked the BAs, the devs not so much.
